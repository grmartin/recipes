# Mom's Mac & Cheese

Preheat oven to 350℉.

## Ingredients

- 2½ cups pasta
- 1½ tsp salt (for pasta water)
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp dry mustard
- dash pepper
- 2 cups shredded cheddar, divided
- 2¼ cups milk
- 3 Tbsp butter, melted
- ½ cup dry bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta 8-9 minutes in water with 1½ tsp salt. Pour into a 13x9 pan or 2½ quart casserole dish.
2. Melt ¼ cup butter in a saucepan, remove from heat. Stir in flour, 1 tsp salt, mustard, and pepper until smooth.
3. Gradually add milk, bringing to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. Add 1¼ cups of the cheese.
4. Layer pasta and ¾ cup cheese; repeat. Sprinkle a little extra cheese on top.
5. Toss melted butter with bread crumbs and sprinkle over the top.
6. Bake uncovered 30 minutes, or until golden.

Serves 6-8.
